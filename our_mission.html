Our Values

Accessible We make workplace education and training accessible by connecting organization to federal and provincial funding programs, and matching them with instructors who are subject matters experts within their field
Accountable We pride ourselves on our accountability and reporting. We stand behind our prequalified talent pool of educators and faciliators and reliable screening mechanism in place to ensure quality
Knowledgeable Our extensive membership of subject matter experts work across many industry sectors and provide a high level of adult education and training best practices
Flexible In addition to offering customized programs tailored to each organization's spcific needs, we provide a diverse group of qualified educators, and make our resources available through flexible class times
Relevant AWENS' members are active in their field of practice, bringing a practical current approach that is relevant to today's workplace
