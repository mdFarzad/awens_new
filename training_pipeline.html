Instructional Pipeline


Make an RFP

AWENS provides organizations looking to submit requests for proposals (RFPs) for education and training the services of qualified adult educators.

Professional Training


At AWENS, we offer a wide range of education and training programs that benefit both individual employees and business owners. As a non-profit organization, we offer the RFP process at no cost to encourage education among Nova Scotians in the workplace.

Our Instructors


The ability to offer quality education and training is a key component of the support and ser vices AWENS provides. In order to reach thousands of workers across the province, AWENS works with over 80 instructors and training consultants. AWENS' certified instructors are screened through a qualification process to ensure they have industr y experience as well as adult education knowledge and skills to deliver programs. Our instructors have diverse backgrounds, with many having earned Masters in Adult Education, Masters in Business Administration, PhD or a technical certification.

  • Score RFPs
  • Conduct interviews

  • Award contract

AWENS is available to assist your organization through the entire RFP process. We can advise you on setting specific goals, what may be required in an instructor, what criteria for scoring are important to you and more.
